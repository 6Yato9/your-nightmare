Hunters in the deep forest regions of Ghana and the Ivory Coast learn to look up. Not for birds or falling branches but for the Sasabonsam, which sits in the high canopy of the oldest trees, its long legs dangling below it, the iron hooks at the ends of its feet hanging at approximately neck or shoulder height for a person walking the forest path below. The hunter who does not look up, who follows the path looking ahead, can walk directly into those hooks without any warning. By the time the hooks have found purchase, the Sasabonsam has begun to pull upward, and what follows is not a struggle that the hunter wins.

The description from Ashanti and surrounding traditions is specific: it stands about two meters tall with an enormous wingspan like a bat's, skin that is red or black, long legs disproportionate to its body, and those iron feet. The mouth is full of teeth and it uses those teeth. It inhabits the deepest and oldest sections of forest where the trees are large enough to support it, and it is most active at night, though it can operate during the day in thick canopy where the light barely reaches the ground.

The Sasabonsam is associated not only with physical attack but with the corruption of medicine men. In Ashanti belief, certain ritual practitioners who have crossed into harmful magic are understood to have established a relationship with the Sasabonsam — using it, being used by it, in a mutual arrangement that gives the practitioner power and gives the creature a defender in the human community. The crooked medicine man and the forest vampire are bound together, which means identifying and sanctioning one requires dealing with the other.

The forest itself changes around a Sasabonsam territory. Trees that the creature regularly uses become marked — the bark is different, the growth patterns are wrong, there is a quality of wrongness to the air. Experienced hunters know these markers. The appropriate response is not engagement but circumnavigation — adding hours to a journey to avoid a single stretch of path is understood, in the communities that hold this knowledge, as the obviously correct choice. The iron hooks in the dark are never worth whatever is on the other side of the shortcut.
